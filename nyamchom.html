NyamChom

NyamChom is a delicacy of the Kenyan people. Often, it is grilled goat meat(mutton) and is often enjoyed whilst having a drink

Ingridients

  • mutton
  • beef
  • kachumbari

Steps

  1. First we get a grill and crank up the fire
  2. Then we prepare our meat, most are plain with no marination
  3. Place the naked meat over the fire
  4. Leave to slowly cook and turning once in a while
  5. For a 'fall of the bone', wrap the meat in aluminum foil and return it on the fire for 20 to 30 mins
  6. Serve with a side of veggies, kachumbari and ugali or chips
